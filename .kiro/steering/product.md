# Product Overview

**Delicious Moments (食光集 / Shiguangji)** is a collaborative family meal planning and grocery list application.

## Core Features

- **Tomorrow's Menu**: Plan breakfast, lunch, and dinner for the family
- **Recipe Library**: Manage and browse family recipes organized by categories
- **Shopping List**: Auto-generate grocery lists from planned meals with ingredient aggregation
- **Memories**: Track cooking history, statistics, and achievements
- **Multi-User**: Support for family members with individual avatars and preferences

## Design Philosophy

The app follows a "Creamy Mochi" design style:
- Soft, rounded corners (rounded-2xl, rounded-3xl)
- Warm color palette (orange primary, green secondary, warm white background)
- Playful, friendly UI with emoji and icons
- Card-based layouts with subtle shadows
- Mobile-first, touch-optimized interface

## Target Users

Families who want to:
- Coordinate meal planning together
- Reduce food waste through organized shopping
- Track dietary habits and achievements
- Share cooking responsibilities
